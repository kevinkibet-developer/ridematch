SWIPE MATCH RIDE

SWIPE

Find your perfect travel companion with a simple swipe. Set your preferences—music taste, chatty or quiet ride, smoking or non-smoking—and browse drivers or passengers heading your way. If someone catches your interest, swipe right! If not, swipe left and keep looking.

MATCH

When you and another traveler both swipe right, it’s a match! You can chat within the app to confirm pickup details, timing, and fare. No awkward negotiations—the app suggests fair pricing, and you can check ratings to ensure a smooth experience.

RIDE

Once everything is set, just show up and enjoy the journey. Track your ride in real-time for safety, and at the end, rate your travel buddy. Whether you’re saving on costs, making new friends, or just getting where you need to go, RideMatch makes every trip better.

Who we are

We Love Our Customers

We are a dynamic carpooling platform that connects riders with drivers for a smooth, affordable, and convenient travel experience. Whether you need a ride or want to offer one, we make every trip comfortable and hassle-free.

RideMatch makes travel more affordable and convenient, allowing passengers to save money while enjoying personalized, comfortable trips. It also promotes an eco-friendly impact by reducing empty car seats, helping cut down on carbon emissions. Beyond savings, the platform fosters safe and social connections, ensuring passengers travel with like-minded people, making rides more enjoyable and less awkward. For drivers, RideMatch offers great perks, helping them offset travel costs while gaining good company on the road.
